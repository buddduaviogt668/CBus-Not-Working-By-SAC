C-Bus Power Supply Failure

If most switches are dead at once, this is usually a failed C-Bus power supply and needs urgent diagnosis.

Call 0422 469 739

Typical Signs

  • Multiple keypads unresponsive together
  • System drops offline intermittently
  • Flashing indicators instead of confirmation
  • Random switching before full outage

What To Do Now

  • Do not repeatedly power-cycle the system
  • Record dead vs partially working zones
  • Book urgent on-site diagnostic
